CYCLADES

IT ALL STARTED IN DELOS. THE REST OF THE ISLANDS "CYRCLED" DELOS TO PAY RESPECT TO THE BIRTHPLACE OF GOD APOLLO AND ARTEMIS. IT WAS ONE OF THE MOST POPULAR SPIRITUAL AND THE CULTURAL CENTERS OF THE ANCIENT GREECE. EVERY ISLAND FORMED INTO A LITTLE CULTURAL GEM, DEVELOPING ITS OWN ARTS AND TRADIITONS. CYCLADES ARE KNOWN FOR THE WHITE HOUSES, BLUE DOMES, BRIGHT SUNLIGHT AND THE UNIQUE AEGEAN LANDSCAPE.

IONIAN ISLANDS

LOCATED ON THE WESTERN COAST OF GREECE WITH LUSH LANDSCAPE AND TURQUISE WATER . THE PLACE THAT IS CONNECTED TO THE JOURNEY OF "ODESSEUS". THE LIFE OF THESE ISLANDS IS SHAPED BY MANY CULTURES, ESPECIALLY THE ITALIAN. CHARMING TOWN ARCHITECTURE AND NATURAL BEAUTY MAKE THIS PLACE A WORLDWIDE DESTINATION.

SPORADES

ACCORDING TO MYTHOLOGY, SPORADES WERE FORMED WHEN THE TITANS THREW MASSIVE ROCKS INTO THE SEA DURING ANCIENT WARS. LINKED TO ACHILLES AND THE SKYRIAN HORSES, SON OF DIONYSOS AND ARIADNE THAT WERE THE FIRST INHABITANTS OF SKOPELOS. FORESTS AND WATERS OF THE NYMPHS AND THE NEREIDS. GASTRONOMY, FESTIVALS, CERAMIC CRAFTS, MARINE PARKS, FILMIMG AREAS, BEAUTIES AND NATURAL LANDSCAPES MAKE THIS BLOCK OF ISLANDS DIVERSE AND COSMOPOLITAN.
