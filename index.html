DIMITRIS MANDILARAS
SAILOR

BORN AND RAIDES ON A GREEK ISLAND MAKES ME A PART OF AN AMBITIOUS COMMUNITY OF YOUNG PEOPLE WHO STAY ACTIVE, SHARE THEIR KNOWLEDGE AND THEIR PASSION ABOUT THEIR CULTURE. I HAVE ALWAYS LIVED BY THE SEA AND I HAVE TURNED THIS INTO A PROFESSION. IT IS INSPIRING TO TRAVEL IN THOSE PLACES THAT HAVE ALWAYS BEEN FULL OF LIFE, MEETING NEW PEOPLE AND SPENDING TIME AT SEA UNDER SAILS. IT IS NOT JUST ISLANDS HOPPING. THROUGH PARTNERS AND FRIENDS AROUND THE ISLANDS, WE EXPLORE EVERY CULTURAL AND TRADITIONAL ASPECT UNDER THHE CYCLADIC SUN.

WELCOME TO MY SAILING PORTFOLIO.

THROUGH THIS SITE YOU CAN LEARN MORE ABOUT MY EXPERIENCE, SEE UNFORGETABLE MOMENTS OF PREVIOUS VOYAGES BUT ALSO BOOK YOUR OWN SAILING TRIP. CAPTIVATING PHOTOGRAPHY AND TRAVEL INFORMATIONS INVITE YOU ONBOARD ON A SAILING TRIP AROUND THE GREEK ISLANDS. MEET THE TRADITIONAL ARCHITECTURE, THE CULTURAL TREASURES AND THE LOCAL CRAFTS. ENJOY THE CRYSTAL CLEAR WATER, THE SUN-KISSED VILLAGES AND THE UNIQUE SUNSETS. WHETHER YOU ARE LOOKING FOR A PROFESSIONAL SAILING CAPTAIN OR GET INSPIRED TO PLAN YOUR SAILING TRIP IN GREECE THIS PLACE IS MADE FOR TRAVELERS AND EXPLORERS.

RESPONSIBLE TRAVEL

TALKING INTO SERIOUS CONS IDARATION OUR POSITIVE ENVIRONMENTAL AND SOCIAL FOOTPRINT WE WILL PREVENT OUR TRIP FROM HAVING NEGATIVE IMPACT AND REMAIN SUSTAINABLE. THIS INCLUDES TRANSPORT EMISSION, ENVIRONMENTAL POLLUSION, LOCAL COMMUNITIES SUPPORT, MANAGE WASTE AND WILDLIFE SIGHTING REPORT. TRAVELLING ON A BOAT CAN BE A LOT LESS SUSTAINABLE THAT MOST PEOPLE WOULD ASSUME. UNDERSTANDING THE IMPACT CAN MAKE IMPROVEMENTS.

PARTNERS

SHARING A COMMON VISION ROUTED IN HERITAGE THE WORK BEGINS WITH THE EXPLORATION OF CULTURAL LIFE. THE AEGEAN SEA SITUATED AT THE CROSSROADS OF EUROPE , ASIA AND AFRICA EMBODIES A BIG DIASPORIC CULTURE AND DEEP TRADITIONAL KNOWLEDGE. THATS THE FOUNDATION OF OUR RESEARCH. STUDY, REINTERPRET AND PRESERVE. THESE ISLANDS FORM A 'LABORATORY FIELD' TO ENGAGE WITH BOATBUILDING, MARITIME CRAFTS, POTTERY AND A WIDE RANGE OF ARTS.
